Biggest Sum

You have to find maximum sum of numbers from top left corner of a matrix to bottom right corner. You can only go down or right.

  1. Matrix will be square M x M, 4 ≤ M ≤ 60
  2. Matrix will have only positive integers N
  3. 10 ≤ N ≤ 200
Example:
Matrix =

[[20, 20, 10, 10],
[10, 20, 10, 10],
[10, 20, 20, 20],
[10, 10, 10, 20]]
